The Problem

Marinas are invisible to the digital world.

Every street and building has a postal code. Millions of boat moorings do not — causing daily confusion for captains, suppliers and marina staff.

  • 🔍
    No standard location formatSuppliers waste 10–15 minutes per delivery searching for the correct berth.
  • ❌
    High operational error rateWrong berth deliveries, missed fuelling and repeated manoeuvres cost money daily.
  • 📵
    Zero digital integrationMarina software and navigation apps cannot reference a berth with precision.
  • 😤
    Frustrating first arrivalCaptains arrive at a marina with no clear guidance to their assigned mooring.

Why sponsors should care

BERTHCODE is not only a marina utility. It is a branded touchpoint at the exact moment when captains, yacht owners, crew and suppliers need help: arrival, service, navigation and berth coordination. The Solution

One code. Exact location. Every time.

BERTHCODE creates a structured, universal naming convention for marina berths — linked to Google Maps and an optional on-berth QR plate.
